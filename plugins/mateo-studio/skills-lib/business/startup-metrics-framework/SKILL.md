# Startup Metrics Framework

Comprehensive guide to tracking, calculating, and optimizing key performance metrics for different startup business models from seed through Series A.

## When to Use

- Working on startup metrics framework tasks or workflows
- Needing guidance, best practices, or checklists for startup metrics
- Analyzing SaaS unit economics, growth metrics, or financial KPIs

## Core SaaS Metrics

### Growth
- **MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue) — Predictable monthly revenue
- **ARR** (Annual Recurring Revenue) — MRR × 12
- **MRR Growth Rate** — Target: 10-15% monthly at early stage
- **New MRR** / **Expansion MRR** / **Churned MRR**

### Acquisition
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost) — Total sales + marketing / new customers
- **Payback Period** — CAC / (ARPU × Gross Margin) — Target: <12 months
- **Conversion Rate** — Visitor → Trial → Paid

### Retention & Revenue
- **Churn Rate** — % customers lost per month — Target: <2-3% monthly
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value) — ARPU / Churn Rate
- **LTV:CAC Ratio** — Target: >3x
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)** — Target: >100% (expansion > churn)
- **Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)** — Target: >85%

### Efficiency
- **Burn Multiple** — Net Burn / Net New ARR — Target: <1.5x
- **Rule of 40** — Growth Rate % + Profit Margin % — Target: >40
- **Magic Number** — Net New ARR / S&M Spend (prior quarter) — Target: >0.75
- **Gross Margin** — Target: >70% for SaaS

## Key Benchmarks by Stage

| Metric | Seed | Series A | Series B |
|--------|------|----------|----------|
| MRR Growth | >15%/mo | >10%/mo | >7%/mo |
| Churn | <5%/mo | <3%/mo | <2%/mo |
| NRR | >90% | >100% | >110% |
| Gross Margin | >60% | >70% | >75% |
| LTV:CAC | >2x | >3x | >4x |
